Tempo Fly

Privacy policy — last updated 2026-05-29

Tempo Fly is a Chrome extension that adjusts playback speed and pitch on music.youtube.com, detects the BPM and key of the current track, and plays short sound effects on top of playback.

What data does Tempo Fly handle?

  • Audio from your active YouTube Music tab — captured briefly (about 12 seconds) only when you click the Detect BPM & key button. The audio is analyzed entirely inside your browser using the Web Audio API. It is never sent to a server, written to disk, or shared with anyone.
  • The current track title and artist — read from the YouTube Music page to label detection results. This stays in your browser.
  • Your preferences — the tempo offset, pitch-preservation toggle, effects volume, and the most recent detection result. Stored with chrome.storage.local on your device.

What Tempo Fly does not do

  • It does not contact any external server.
  • It does not include analytics, tracking, or advertising.
  • It does not access any site other than music.youtube.com.
  • It does not collect identifiers, account information, or browsing history.
  • It does not sell, share, or transmit user data to third parties.

Permissions, plainly

Granted at install:

  • storage — remember your preferences and last detection result locally.
  • Host permission for https://music.youtube.com/* — the extension only operates on YouTube Music.

Requested only when you click Detect BPM & key:

  • tabCapture — capture audio from the YouTube Music tab during the on-demand BPM/key analysis. Released as soon as analysis completes.
  • offscreen — run the audio analysis in an offscreen document, off the service worker thread.

You can revoke either of the optional permissions at any time from Chrome's extension settings without uninstalling Tempo Fly. Tempo, pitch, and effects will continue to work; only detection will stop until you grant them again.

Children's privacy

Tempo Fly does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated date at the top of this page will reflect the change. The extension's privacy practices will not be quietly broadened.
